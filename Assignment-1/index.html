About FC Fassell

Founded in 2009, FC Fassell is a professional football club based in Monrovia, Liberia. Known as "The Soccer Missionaries", we are recognized for our dedication to excellence, young talent development, and rapid ascent in Liberian football.

Our commitment extends beyond the pitch, as we strive to inspire and develop the next generation of Liberian football stars while contributing to the growth of the sport in our community.
